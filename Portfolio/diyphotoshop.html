Abstract Canvas

Medium: Digital Art - coded with JavaScript, Dimensions: 1600 x 1500

A DIY photoshop up to 11 brushes to create your own dreamy abstract illustrations through bits of mixing the brushes together.
From giving your shapes outlines as you paint drag around, switching the color for brush 5 through painting a bit with brush 0
then going back to brush 5. The endless possibilities is up for messing around like a dream.
