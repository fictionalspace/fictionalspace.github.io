Whimisical Flavors

Medium: Digital Art - Procreate, Code - Ren'py, python, Dimensions: 1080 x 1920

Whimisical Flavors is the cooking game in which you either follow instructions or straight up poison the chef.
Play as the apprentice of the chef and help cook meals for the chef to taste test.

Concept

I wanted to create a cooking game reminiscent of flash cooking games, the initial idea
was depending on the flavors that used to create the meal it would influence the chef character.
Going off too much of one thing could be an overdose on emotions and is up to the player to
strike a balance between indulging yet grounding back to reality.
Yet this idea ended up being quite complex and all over the place to figure out so the concept
was tuned down to cooking meals either barebones, bad, or a well balanced meal for the chef to
taste test. Honing in the message of self-care how despite how life can feel like on loop and
repetition or even your own negative emotions end up overwhelming you. Sometimes treating
yourself with some kindness is the least you can make a difference for yourself whether you
think it matters or not in the moment. Yet ensuring your physical body is able to eat well enough
and take care of yourself helps a little along the way. Or times when ourselves might even hurt
ourselves by denying ourselves of good meals. People are complicated so why not try to make a
visual novel game about that!
